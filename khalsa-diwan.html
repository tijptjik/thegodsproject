Khalsa Diwan

Thu 10 November 2016

Episode 5

Like a drop of water longing for its source, we are keen to be made whole again with God, according to the Sikh faith. In today’s conversations, we find out more about God’s essence and universal nature from a teacher and translator who worship at Khalsa Diwan on Queen's Road East.
